Vision

>

Shared waste collection areas (apartments, campuses, institutions, etc.) often face garbage overflow, delayed disposal, and safety risks such as gas buildup or fire hazards due to the lack of real-time monitoring.

>

There is no integrated system that combines garbage level monitoring, air quality and fire hazard detection, user and waste bin/dumping zone location tracking, and alerts in a single platform.

>

Hence, there is a need for a smart, automated, and real-time waste monitoring system that can detect garbage levels, monitor air quality, and notify authorities promptly to ensure cleanliness and public health safety.
